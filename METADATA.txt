--------------------------
DATA-SPECIFIC INFORMATION
--------------------------

Number of variables: 19

Number of cases/rows: 3406

List of variables:

		subBasin: subbasin unique ID based on the Watershed Boundary Dataset (HUC8 level). Continuous
		ET_resilienceExtABOVE: resilience to water losses. Continuous
		ET_resilienceExtBELOW: resilience to productivity losses. Continuous
		ecoregionLevelIII: ecoregion unique ID based on the North American Terrestrial Ecoregions-Level III dataset. Categorical
		ecoregionIII_ID: ecoregion unique ID. A sequence from 1 to 84. Categorical
		MaximumTemperature: interannual mean maximum air temperature for the period 1950-2017 (rows 1-1703) and 2018-2099 (rows 1704-3406). Continuous
		MinimumTemperature: interannual mean minimum air temperature for the period 1950-2017 (rows 1-1703) and 2018-2099 (rows 1704-3406). Continuous
		Precipitation: interannual mean precipitation for the period 1950-2017 (rows 1-1703) and 2018-2099 (rows 1704-3406). Continuous
		Runoff: interannual mean runoff for the period 1950-2017 (rows 1-1703) and 2018-2099 (rows 1704-3406). Continuous
		SnowWaterEquivalent: interannual mean snow water equivalent for the period 1950-2017 (rows 1-1703) and 2018-2099 (rows 1704-3406). Continuous
		SoilWaterStorage: interannual mean soil water storage for the period 1950-2017 (rows 1-1703) and 2018-2099 (rows 1704-3406). Continuous
		EvaporativeDeficit: interannual mean evaporative deficit for the period 1950-2017 (rows 1-1703) and 2018-2099 (rows 1704-3406). Continuous
		deciduousCover: percentage of area covered by deciduous forest per subbasin in 2016. Continuous
		evergreenCover: percentage of area covered by evergreen forest per subbasin in 2016. Continuous
		mixedCover: percentage of area covered by mixed forest per subbasin in 2016. Continuous
		numbAboveAnomal: number of extreme positive anomalies. Continuous
		AboveIntensity: mean of extreme positive anomalies. Continuous
		numbBelowAnomal: number of extreme negative anomalies. Continuous
		BelowIntensity: mean of extreme negative anomalies. Continuous
   
The last 14 variables are standardized to have a mean of zero and standard deviation of 1.
